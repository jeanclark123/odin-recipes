Eggplant Lasagna

Description

A different take on this traditional dish, where eggplant replaces pasta for a delicious, mouthwatering, meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon of olive oil for brushing
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup Italian-seasoned bread crumbs
  • salt and ground pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggplants , peeled and sliced into 1/2 inch rounds
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 48 ounces chunky tomato sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Directions

Step 1

Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease 2 baking sheets and a 9x13 baking dish with 1 teaspoon olive oil.

Step 2

Whisk together eggs and water in a shallow dish. Combine Parmesan cheese, bread crumbs, salt and pepper in a separate shallow dish.

Step 3

Dip eggplant slices into egg mixture, then press into bread and crumb mixture. Gently tap off any excess crumbs. Arrange breaded eggplant slices on the prepared baking sheets.

Step 4

Bake in the preheated oven until the bottoms are brown, 20 to 25 minutes. Flip eggplant and continue baking until tender, 20 to 25 minutes. Set aside.

Step 5

Increase the oven temperature to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C)

Step 6

Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a skiller over medium-heat Stir beef, season with salt and pepper. Cook and stir unti beef is brown and crumbly, about 10 mins. Drain excess grease. Stir tomato sause into ground beeg, bring to a simmer, then set aside.

Step 7

Place a 1/3 of the eggplant slices on the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Pour 1/3 of the meat sauce on top of the eggplant layer.

Step 8

Sprinkle 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese on top of the sauce layer. Repeat this step 2 more times, finishing with a layer of mozzarella cheese.

Step 9

Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and sauce is bubbling, 10-15 minutes. Cool for 5 minutes before slicing.
